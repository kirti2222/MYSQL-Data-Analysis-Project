# MYSQL-Data-Analysis-Project
This data analysis project is about the health activity data with the information including Id,Age,Gender,Height,Weight,daily steps,calories intake,hours of sleep,heart rate,BMI,Blood Pressure, exercise per hour,smoker,alcohol consumption,diabetic and heart disease.The tool used for the analysis is MYSQL and is analyzed through MYSQL queries. The objective of of this analysis is to explore the health activity data of individuals based on their daily habbits related to their health and how health is related to these daily activities and whether this data can be used to understand the improvement in the individuals's health conditions or not.
Key Findings of this analysis are as follows:
1. The average age of all individuals in the dataset is 49.
2. This data represents 523 male individuals and 477 female individuals.
3. The minimum, maximum and average daily steps of individuals are 1016, 19931 and 10717.
4. The minimum, maximum and average hours of sleep are 4 hours, 10 hours and 6 hours.
5. The minimum, maximum and average calories intake are 1201.3498 and 2327.
6. The number of individuals who are smokers are 191 and non-smokers are 809.
7. The average BMI of all the individuals is 26.
8. The daily steps of males and females is 10960 and 10450.
9. The average hours of sleep for adults, seniors and young adults is 6 hours.
10. The average BMI for individuals based on thier daily steps activity levels such as High, Low and Medium are 26.69,26.78 and 26.78.
11. The average heart rate of individuals who have heart disease is 83.68 and those individuals who do not have heart disease is 84.82.
12. The percentage of smokers who have heart disease is 7.85340 and non-smokers who have heart disease is 9.64153 which is interestingly higher than smokers percentage.
